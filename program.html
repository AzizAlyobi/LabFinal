National day of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia Program


7:30pm Starting to allow the invitees to enter the hall
8:00pm Reciting the national anthem
here the concert will begin with the saying of the national anthem by the attendees.
8:10pm The first performer is Rashid Al Majid
Here the party will start with the great artist Rashid Al Majid, one of the top Saudi singers, and he answered our invitation to sing in this important concert, and he will have 45 minutes to sing as the artist will have the opportunity to sing the songs agreed upon and not exceed the time specified for him.
9:00pm The second performer, Hussein Al Jasmi
Hussein Al Jasmi is a great Emirati artist who came to share our joy on our great day and prepared many songs for this concert, and also 45 minutes were set for him in order to hear his wonderful and beautiful voice.
9:55am The third performer, Ayed Youssef
Ayed is a young rising artist with a beautiful voice, and he is coming strong to the singing scene, and we saw that it was appropriate to invite him to sing and we are sure that he will perform a wonderful performance.
10:00pm The fourth performer, Abadi Al-Jawhar
The last performer before the honoring performers, is the legendary Abadi Al-Jawhar, one of the greatest Saudi and Arab singers who came to hear his wonderful voice and share with us this great night.
11:00pm

Honoring the present performers and media professionals
Media journalist Reem Al-Najm
Here, the performers and the media attending will be honored, and the goal of this thing is to thank them for their presence and their participation with us in our joy, which is a shield of honor with their names.

Noon Dinner
1:00am Fifth performer, Dalia Mubarak
Dalia Mubarak has a wonderful and luxurious voice and she is the only female singer participating. The party is a young artist coming strongly to the singing scene. We hope that the audience enjoys her beautiful voice and she has prepared a number of her beautiful songs for us.
1:55am The last performer, Mohamed Abdo
Muhammad Abdo is one of the greatest singing voices in the world and he is an artist loved by everyone and he is called the Arab artist. He came to share our joy with this great day, the day our great country was founded. The great artist has prepared for us many wonderful songs. We hope the attendees enjoy his beautiful voice.
3:00am Duet among all performers
Here all the singers have gathered in order to present a collective song on the occasion of this great day on which our beloved kingdom was founded. This song is distinguished by the different layers of voices of the wonderful singers written by the great, veteran poet Badr bin Abdul Mohsen, and we hope that our surprises will impress you.
3:30am End of the concert.
