National Day Event

The National Day of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is a great day. On this day, this great country in which we were born and lived was unified and established, and it is a day in which all citizens, women and men, old and young, rejoice. Because of this great limit, the Entertainment Authority held this special party for the National Day in King Abdullah Sports City in the city Jeddah, on the 23th of September, to celebrate the New Year of our great country. We are promised a big party that includes many great artists and media
